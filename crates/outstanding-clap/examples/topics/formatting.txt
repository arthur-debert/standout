TEXT FORMATTING
---------------
The sample app supports basic text formatting:

  *bold*      -> Bold text
  _italic_    -> Italic text
  `code`      -> Inline code

COLORS
------
Terminal colors are automatically detected and applied
based on your terminal capabilities.

TIPS
----
1. Use short titles for better display in lists
2. First line is always the title
3. Blank line separates title from content
